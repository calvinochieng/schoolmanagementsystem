Addressing Real Educational Challenges in Kenya

Effective discipline management in schools remains a significant challenge worldwide, particularly in the absence of timely communication between educators and parents. Traditional systems of reporting misconduct often fail to provide timely intervention opportunities.

This system addresses the critical gap identified in current discipline management practices, where manual reporting and delayed communication hinder effective student behavior intervention.

The Problem

Many Kenyan schools still rely on monthly reports and occasional parent meetings, creating delays in addressing misconduct and reducing accountability opportunities for students.
